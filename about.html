Techmach is a general engineering company in the agri sector, with over 30 years of experience. The company provides high-quality products and services to the food industry, including machinery and components, turnkey projects, and project design. Supported by energetic management, skilled personnel, and investment in productive equipment, Techmach continues to strengthen its design and manufacturing facilities to align with world-class standards of quality and productivity.
