About Techmach

A short company overview.

Company Description

Techmach is a general engineering company in the Agri sector, with over 30 years of experience. The company is dedicated to becoming Africa's leading engineering company, specializing in cereal milling, animal feed milling, seed processing, and other related fields. Techmach is supported by its young and energetic management, investment in productive equipment, and skilled personnel. Techmach provides high-quality products and services to the food industry, including machinery and components, turnkey projects and project design. The company has made substantial investments in design and manufacturing facilities to match world standards of quality and productivity.
